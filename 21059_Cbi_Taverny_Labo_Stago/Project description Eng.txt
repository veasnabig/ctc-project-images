22011 : LABORATORY STAGO

The construction project of a laboratory and offices proximity for the company STAGO.

The laboratory is 6200 m2 on 2 super floors with a semi-buried and full basement level in relation to the superstructure, for parking use. The roof is made of a steel structure. The project also provides for the installation of roads and parking spaces around the future building.


Location	:	Taverny, France
Our Mission	:	BIM service, Structural design and Shop drawings
Owner	:	STAGO
Architect/Consultant	:  Kardham Architecture	
Builder	:	CBI Campenon Bernard Industrie
